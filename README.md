# MealMaster

**Plan the week like an app, not a sticky note.**

MealMaster is a personal meal planning web app built for people who cook for themselves and want to stop reinventing the wheel every week. It solves a specific problem: spending too much mental energy deciding what to cook, what groceries to buy, and how to avoid ingredients going to waste.

Live app: [mealmaster-nine-umber.vercel.app](https://mealmaster-nine-umber.vercel.app)

---

## The problem it solves

Every week: what do I actually know how to cook? What do I have ingredients for? If I buy cauliflower this week, will I use it before it goes bad?

Grocery runs were taking more planning than they should. MealMaster brings the recipe library, weekly planner, recommendation engine, and grocery list into one place.

---

## Features

**Recipe Library**\
Save the recipes you actually make, not aspirational ones you will never cook. Tag by meal type, dietary preference, or prep time. Search by recipe name, ingredient, or tag.

**Smart Recommendations**\
Tell the app how many portions you need this week, how many unique recipes you want, your max prep time, and your preference mode (balanced, fast, or variety). The recommendation engine surfaces recipes from your saved library that share ingredients, so nothing goes to waste.

**Weekly Planner**\
Assign recommended meals to specific days. Each day supports multiple meals and you can adjust portions per assignment. Navigate between weeks to plan ahead.

**Grocery List**\
Automatically generated from your weekly plan. Organized by category (Produce, Dairy, Pantry, Frozen, Protein, Spices) so your grocery run is actually efficient. Shows which recipe each ingredient is needed for and in what quantity.

**Shared Prep Opportunities**\
When ingredients overlap across planned meals, MealMaster surfaces prep wins so you can batch cook once and use across multiple days.
